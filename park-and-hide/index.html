The Game

Wander. Settle in. Stay unseen.

You've inherited a camper van from Walter, a legendary stealth camper, along with his life on the road. Where you take it from there is up to you.

Find tonight's spot

Every location hides places to spend the night. Some are safe and quiet. Some are risky, ridiculous, and absolutely worth it.

Make it to morning

The longer you stay, the more attention you draw. Manage your lights, your noise, and your nerve until the sun comes up.

Live out of your van

Craft and place furniture, decorate your space, and upgrade your rig. Your van is your home, and it grows with you.

Meet the locals

Every stop has its people, each with their own story that unfolds the more you visit. Some of them might even change your life.

Explore the coast and beyond

Windswept cliffs, pine forests, city streets after dark. Each place has its own rhythm, resources, and secrets.

Cozy stakes, always

Getting caught is a setback, never a game over. You wake up, you keep your van, and the road is always there in the morning.
