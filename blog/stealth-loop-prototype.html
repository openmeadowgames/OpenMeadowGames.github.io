Stealth Camping Loop Prototype Complete

June 5, 2026 Development Update

Big milestone this week: the core gameplay loop of Park & Hide exists and is playable start to finish. It's all grey boxes and programmer art, but the heart of the game is beating.

The Loop

One full cycle plays out like this:

  • Site selection — arrive somewhere new and scout for a spot to spend the night. Some spots are safe and boring, others are risky but rewarding.
  • Gear loadout — decide what you set up for the evening. Everything that makes the night more comfortable also makes you easier to notice.
  • Stealth rolls — as the night passes, the game checks whether anyone spots you. Risk builds the longer you stay in one place, so pushing your luck is a real decision.
  • Dawn resolution — make it to morning unseen and you're rewarded for the clean night. Get caught and you lose something, pack up, and drive on.

Cozy Stakes

The important design rule: getting caught is a setback, never a game over. This is a cozy game at its core. The tension of hoping nobody knocks on your van at 2 a.m. is the spice, not the whole meal. You always wake up, you always keep your van, and the road is always there in the morning.

Why This Matters

Everything else I have planned — the locations, the locals and their stories, decorating the RV, upgrading your van — hangs off this loop. Now that it holds together, every system I add from here makes an already-playable game better rather than being a bet on something unproven.

Thanks for following the journey. More updates soon.
