Ricardo's Shelter Has a Home

June 17, 2026 Development Update

Smaller session today, mostly cleanup and groundwork rather than flashy new features. Here's what shipped.

Ricardo's Shelter Has a Home

Ricardo and his dog shelter are now wired into the Rest Stop. He wanders near the antique store with a small placeholder sprite standing in for his character art, and the shelter itself is also placeholder for now, just a stand-in building with a fenced run out back so the space reads correctly. Both pieces will swap out cleanly once final art lands; nothing about how Ricardo talks or remembers your visits needed to change.

Tileset Refresh

Swapped the old numbered tileset pack (1. Grass and dirt.png through 9. Beach.png) for a proper Beach tileset and a Large Cliff/Sand/Dirt/Grass set. Cleaner naming, more tiles to work with for the coastal areas.
