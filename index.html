The Studio

Small studio,
big open world.

Open Meadow Games is an independent game studio based in Vancouver, British Columbia. We create cozy, exploration-forward games that feel like stepping into a warm, unhurried world.


Founded in 2026 by a solo developer with 17 years of game industry experience, we believe the best games give you space to breathe, wander, and discover something unexpected around every corner.
